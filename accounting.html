Accounting Tutorials

Crete an invoice

  1. Open Sales module.
  2. Remove the "My Quotation" filter from search bar.
  3. Search for the Sales Order that relates to the invoice to be created.
  4. Click on the sales order.
  5. Click the create invoice button.
  6. Click create and view invoice.
  7. Review Invoice.
  8. Click confirm.
  9. Invoice has been successfully created.

Create one invoice from multiple sales orders (Same customer).

  1. Open the sales module.
  2. Click on the to invoice tab.
  3. Click on orders to invoice.
  4. Click the checkboxes beside sales order that should be merged to create a single invoice.
  5. Click on the action button.
  6. Click on the create invoices option.
  7. Click on the create and view invoice button.
    8. .
  8. Review the invoice.
  9. Click on the confirm button.
  10. Invoice has been successfully created from multiple invoices.

Create a bill

  1. Open the accounting module.
  2. Select the purchase order to be used to reference bill.
  3. Click the create bill button.
  4. Click the edit button.
  5. Enter the relevant fields (Bill Date etc.)
  6. Review the bill.
  7. Click the save button
  8. Click the confirm button.
  9. Bill has been successfully created.

Merge multiple vendor bills

  1. Open the purchasing module
  2. Search by vendor name.
  3. Identify purchase orders that bills should be merged from.
  4. Select one of the purchae orders
  5. Click create bill.
  6. Click edit.
  7. Fill the auto-complete field with the other purchase order number and press enter.
  8. Enter the bill date.
  9. Click confirm.
  10. Click the save button.
  11. Bills have successfully been merged.

Print customer statement (recievables listing)

  1. Open the contacts module
  2. Search for the customer for which report is wanted.
  3. Select customer.
  4. Select the due amount.
  5. Click print letter.
  6. Save customer statement.
  7. Customer statement has been successfuly saved.

Register Payment on an invoice

  1. Open the accounting module.
  2. Click on the customer invoices tab.
  3. Select the invoice payment is to be applied to.
  4. Click on the register payment button.
  5. Fill out all relevant field related to the payment.
  6. Click on create payment.
  7. Payment has been successfully registered. The invoice is now in payment.

Register payment on vendor bill.

  1. Open the accounting module.
  2. Open the vendor bills tabs.
  3. Select the bill payment is to be applied to.
  4. FIll in all relevant fields (Journal, Amount, Payment Date, Memo etc.)
  5. Click the create payment button.
  6. Payment has successfully been applied to vendor bill. The bill is now in payment.

Register payment without invoice

  1. Open the accounting module.
  2. Click the customers tab.
  3. Click on the payments tab.
  4. Click on the create button.
  5. Enter all the relevant information (Journal, Customer, Amount, Date, Memo etc.)
  6. Click the confirm button.
  7. Payment has been registered.

Register unapplied payment to multiple invoices

  1. Open the accounting module.
  2. Open the customer tab.
  3. Click on payments.
  4. Selct the unapplied payment to be used.
  5. Click the edit button.
  6. Enter the amount to be applied to each invoice in the reconcile amount column.
  7. Click the reconcile button.
  8. Click the save button
  9. Payment has been successfully applied to invoices.

General reconciliation of payments.

  1. Open the accounting modules.
  2. Click the reconcile on the jornal for respective reconcilation.
  3. Search for vendor/customer to which payment has been registered.
  4. Select transaction (invoice/vendor bill) to be reconciled
  5. Click on the validate button.
  6. Invoice/Vendor Bill has been updated from in pament to paid.
