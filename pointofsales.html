Point of Sales Tutorials

Open New Session

  1. Open the point of sales module.
  2. Click on new session that corresponds with your location.
  3. Click on the select cashier button.
  4. Select your name on the cashier listing.
  5. Enter your pin.
  6. Click the ok button.
  7. Click the calculator button.
  8. Enter the breakdown of the float received at the start of the session.
  9. Click the confirm button.
  10. Click the open session button.
  11. New session has been successfully opened.

Continue ongoing session

  1. Open the point of sales module.
  2. Click the continue selling button that corresponds with your location.
  3. Click the select cashier button.
  4. Select your name on the cashier list.
  5. Enter your PIN.
  6. Click the ok button.
  7. You have successfully logged into an ongoing session.

Process New Sale

  1. Open new session or continue ongoing session.
  2. Select product from listing or search for required product.
  3. Click the quantity button.
  4. Enter the quanity of the selected product.
  5. Repeat step 2-4 for all products needed by customer.
  6. When all products have been added, click the payment button.
  7. Select the payment method being used by the customer. Multiple payment methods can be used.
  8. Enter the amount to be applied by each payment method.
  9. Only if the cashier is serving an existing cutomer: Click the customer button.
  10. Only if the cashier is serving an existing cutomer: Select the customer being served.
  11. Only if the cashier is serving an existing cutomer: Click the set customer button.
  12. Click the validate button.
  13. Click the print receipt button.
  14. New sale has been successfully processed

Overwrite prices in POS (Only Administrators)

  1. Open new session or continue exxisting session.
  2. Select product from listing or search for product in search bar.
  3. Select the price button.
  4. Enter the correct price.
  5. Proceed with sale.

Customer payments on invoices from POS

  1. Open new session or continue ongoing session.
  2. Click the customer button.
  3. Search for customer in search bar.
  4. Click the shopping cart button for corresponding customer.
  5. Select payment method being used by customer.
  6. Enter the amount of money being applied through each payment method.
  7. Click the validate button.
  8. Click the yes button.
  9. The payment has been successfully to customer's invoices.

Refund customer order

  1. Open new session or continue ongoing session.
  2. Click the refund button.
  3. Click the order to be refunded.
  4. Select the product and quanity to be refunded.
  5. Click the refund button.
  6. Click the payment button.
  7. Select the payment method to be used to refund order.
  8. Enter amount of money to be applied by each payment method.
  9. Click the validate button.
  10. Click the print receipt button.
  11. Refund has been properly processed and documented.

Close Session

  1. Open new session or continue existing session.
  2. Click the close button.
  3. Enter the amount counted for each payment method.
  4. Click the close session button.
  5. Session has been sucessfully closed.
