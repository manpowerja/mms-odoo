Purchasing Tutorials

Creating a purchase order.

  1. Open in the purchasing module.
  2. Click create to create purchase order.
  3. Fill out the rest the relevant feilds (Vendor, Deliver To, Currency etc.)
  4. Add products to purchase order.
  5. Fill out all relevant fields for each product (product, quanity, unit of measurement)
  6. Save the purchase order.
  7. Send purchase order for approval.
  8. Once purchase order has been approved, the purchase order has been successfully created.

Creating a bill.

  1. Select purchase order that bill should be created from.
  2. Click on create bill.
  3. Fill out all relevant fields (Bill Reference, Bill Date). The bill reference is the invoice number received from the vendor.
  4. Confirm the bill.
  5. Bill has been successfully created.
