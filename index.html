Tutorials for Odoo Platform

Sales and Distibution

Sales and Distribution Odoo Platform was launched on February 1, 2023. This Enterprise Resource Planning platform was aimed at encompassing all operations of the company. This website is constructed to assist employees of Sales and Distribution to properly navigate the Odoo Platform.

Purchasing
These tutorials are regarding all stock that is purchased by the sales and distribution procurment department. This entails creating purchase orders, creating bills and send purchase orders for approvals.
Accounting
These tutorials are regarding all procedures that are completed by the accounting division within the Odoo Platform. These procedures entails duties of the payables, collection and general accounitng deparments.
Sales
Being the major function of the company, sales play a vital rule in the daily operations of Sales & Distribution. These procedures are completed by sales representatives and members of various inventory departments.
Point of Sales
The point of sale used at the clean store location are another vital aspect of the Sales and Distribution company. These tutorials entail regular sales, refunds and allowing customer to pay for invoices from the POS.
Inventory
This module entails all movement of products that take place in Sales and Distribution. This requires a great level of responsibility and attention. These tutorials entail recipts, delivery orders, internal transfers and returns.
Administration
This module encompasses all routine tasks that complete by administrators within the Odoo Platform. Tutorials present in this module are creating journals, adding users, adding employees etc.
